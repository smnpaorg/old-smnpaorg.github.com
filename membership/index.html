Membership

Members of SMNPA agree to promote and support the mission and values of the Smoky Mountain Native Plants Association.

Benefits include:

  • Discounts to educational events and workshop
  • Participation in research
  • Vendor space at the Spring Plant Sale and Farmers Market
  • Regional Calendar of Events and newsletter

SMNPA meets the 1st and 3rd Thursday of each month, from 7-9 PM at the Stecoah Valley Center, a historic Appalachian Cultural Arts Facility. The address is 121 Schoolhouse Road, Stecoah, North Carolina 28771.

Please call 828-479-8788 for more information.
